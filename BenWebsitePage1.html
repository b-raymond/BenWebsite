Peanutbutter and Jelly Sandwich

Peanutbutter and jelly sandwiches have always been my favorite type of easily made sandwich.
That being said, I thought it would be helpful for some if I wrote up this tutorial on how to make one.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanutbutter
  • Jelly

Method

  1. Put 2 slices of bread on a plate
  2. Open peanutbutter and jelly jars
  3. Use a knife to spread the jelly on one of the slices of bread
  4. Clean off the knife (optional)
  5. Use the knife to spread the peanutbutter on the other slice
  6. Put one slice on top of the other, making sure the faces with the condiments on them meet
  7. You're done! Make sure to chew before swallowing!
